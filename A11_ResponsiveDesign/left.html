WLOP

greatest digital artist of all time

When you search for “modern art” you’ll find yourself looking at a collection of abstract art, because in 2022 these terms are interchangeable. In this sense, modern art has become a joke, one that’s lacking both meaning and punchline. However, this should not be the case. Art in the modern era has a whole lot more to it than non-objective and vague blobs of paint on a canvas. Art has become revolutionized and has embodied a new form of presence, one so powerful that it's shaken the future of traditional art. I’m referring to digital media. As you know, the internet has taken the world into its grasp with no signs of letting go. Although other people may disregard digital art as not “real art”, it’s clear that between the two mediums one requires more work. Take the artist Wang Ling, more widely known as WLOP, for example. WLOP—a hobbyist digital artist based in China and creator of three original series: “GhostBlade”, “DeadDreams”, and “Cloud Insect”—is a magnificent painter whose skills are not to be overlooked. Every one of his digital illustrations demonstrate complete mastery and thorough comprehension of utilizing art principles. Here is just a snippet of what WLOP is capable of:

Fragile2 (2022)

How the figure is positioned leaves us a compelling impression, the way the light cascade into the monumental building and bounces off the metal from the armor, how the background light contrasts with the main subject’s lament pallet, how your attention gets slowly diverted to the middle ground where the crowd of knight’s appear in awe of the scene, how the addition of the misty fog in the air protrudes a sorrowful and mysterious atmosphere.

Just staring at the amount of detail that WLOP has displayed in the background already sets him levels apart from other artists. WLOP elegantly composes all these artistic elements together, using the years he spent refining his style and craftsmanship, and only then is a masterpiece created.

Compared to other pieces of “art”, WLOP’s illustrations are more captivating to the eye because of his impressive display of expertise in the field. In terms of art, it's clear which genre requires more dexterity to procure. Whereas abstract art is drawn from one’s spirituality and emotional state of mind, digital media requires not only expertise in the art field but also enough comprehension of technology to effectively apply it onto a digital software.
